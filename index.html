Ivan Chermayeff

Graphic Designer

Life and Photos of Ivan Chermayeff:

Life #1

  • Created many of the logos that we know of today.
  • Died at age 85, born June 6, 1932 and died on December 4, 2017.
  • Known for his sleek modern designs with bold colors.
  • He made his logos, "clean, crisp and instantly comprehensible".

Life #2

  • Grew up in a family of architects and artists.
  • Studied at Harvard University, the Institute of Design, Illinois Institute of Technology, and the Yale University School of Design.
  • Aside from logos, he also designed posters, created museum and gallery displays and illustrated children's books.

Quotes From Ivan

  • "clean, crisp and instantly comprehensible"
  • "Design is directed toward human beings. To design is to solve human problems by identifying them and executing the best solution."
  • "Styles come and go, design goes on forever: solving communication problems with new tools applied to the same old common sense."
  • "Good design, at least part of the time, includes the criterion of being direct in relation to the problem at hand - not obscure, trendy, or stylish. A new language, visual or verbal, must be couched in a language that is already understood."
